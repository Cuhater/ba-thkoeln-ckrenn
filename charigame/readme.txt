EDG is a versatile fundraising plugin designed to streamline your donation campaigns with ease. With the ability to run multiple campaigns concurrently, this plugin empowers you to manage diverse fundraising initiatives effortlessly.

## == Description ==


Key Features:

-   **Multiple Campaign Management:** Run and manage various campaigns simultaneously, allowing you to engage with donors across different causes seamlessly.
-   **SMTP Email Integration:** Utilize your configured SMTP server for reliable email communication, ensuring that your campaign updates and donor correspondence reach recipients efficiently.
-   **Customizable Games:** Incorporate a range of games into your campaigns, offering interactive experiences for donors. Tailor settings for each game to suit your campaign's specific objectives.
-   **User Import Functionality:** Simplify user management by importing users directly into the system, enabling quick and efficient donor registration and engagement.
-   **Pipedrive Integration:** Seamlessly integrate with Pipedrive to streamline donor relationship management. Keep track of interactions and transactions with donors, ensuring effective donor engagement and follow-up.
-   **User Overview Dashboard:** Gain insights into your donor base with a comprehensive overview dashboard. Track donor activity, contributions, and engagement levels to inform strategic decision-making and campaign optimization.

## == Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.
1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
